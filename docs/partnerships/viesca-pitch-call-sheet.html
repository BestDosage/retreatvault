Lead with this The Killer Insight

They are mid-pivot into wellness. Spa La Corte opened 2025 (whirlpool, Turkish bath, sauna, Spa Suite). Holistic program — yoga, breathwork, sound healing with Debra Kochanczyk. Owners' stated aim: "making Viesca a holistic retreat for body and mind."

👉 You're not selling traffic. You're installing them in the category they're spending capital to enter.
The 4-night ask is easy for them: they already run a "book 4 nights, 4th night free" promo + August discounts. They have soft periods — the comp costs them nearly nothing on unsold nights.

Cheat sheet Objection Handling

"What's your traffic actually worth to us?"
"15K/month today, high-intent Americans comparing retreats. Fully transparent — monthly UTM-tracked reports. If it doesn't perform, the pilot ends at 6 months and it cost you four unsold room-nights."
"Why should we be exclusive with you?"
Flip it: "The exclusivity runs the other way — we're exclusive to you. Keep every other channel. We recommend only Viesca in Europe."
"We need brand / content approval." (Ferragamo)
"Built in. Nothing publishes without your sign-off. You also get a license to reuse the film and photography in your own channels."
"Why not just pay a media rate like press?"
"Press gives you one article. We're building a permanent sales channel — landing page, video, top placement — with monthly reporting. A channel partnership, not a press stay."
"We need to check with Il Borro / ownership."
"Understood — who should be on the follow-up? I'll send a one-pager you can forward today." ← Get the name.
"Can you guarantee bookings?"
Never. "I can guarantee placement, production quality, and transparent reporting. Bookings follow intent — and our audience is intent."

Guardrails Do NOT Say

  • Don't state 85K/mo as fact — it's a projection; they can check SimilarWeb.
  • No promises of "#1 on Google" or specific booking volumes.
  • No money talk — no affiliate %, no fees. Comp-for-content only; monetization at renewal.
  • Don't trash OTAs or their partners (SLH, Virtuoso) — you're an additive direct channel.
  • Don't accept "sounds interesting, send us something" without a next step + date.
